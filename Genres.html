Popular Genres Of Music

Experts categorize music to two broad parts:


  1. Western Music
  2. Eastern Music

These two broad genres(parts/regions) of music are the divided into countless different genres ranging from some intergenre music like Rock Music and Pop to other less known genres which are mostly regional but are included in these two broad genres for example, country music or khorasani music.
